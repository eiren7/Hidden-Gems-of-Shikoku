Find the solitude of your own.

Deep in Shikoku Mountains, there are countless tranquil landscape waiting to be explored by adventurous travellers. One such hidden gem is located in Kamikatsu, Tokushima, the smallest town of Shikoku.

The Tsurugi Mountain Super Forest Road, the longest forest road in Japan, winds its way through the mountains alongside the Katsuura River. This beautiful river originates from the mountains and offers a stunning backdrop to the surrounding forests and mountains.
